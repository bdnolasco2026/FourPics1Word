Review the Pictures

The clue will be given in the form of 4 pictures. Review the pictures and find out what they have in common.

Formulate your Word

Once you've thought about what they have in common, formulate a word using only the given letters.

Enter your guess

Tap each letter of the word in order to move the letters to the squares. If you change your mind about a letter, tap the backspace to move it back to the list.
